We are finding our groove with designing and producing custom furniture like this 51" x 92" custom resin river table. We worked directly with our client and pull her imagination into reality!

Sourced from a local Denver Colorado based hardwood dealer, this table was literally fresh out of the kiln. I made this table from two Russian Olive slabs that are from the same tree. They were the 3rd and 4th cuts of the tree.

In all, this table consumed 12 full gallons of [Total Boat Deep Fathom](https://www.totalboat.com/products/thickset-fathom-deep-pour-epoxy) epoxy. Additionally, it used just about 200 grams of [Eye Candy Pigments Dark Ocean Green](https://eyecandypigments.com/products/dark-ocean-green?_pos=1&_sid=3ae3d0c91&_ss=r) mica powder.

I finished this table with [Rubio Monocoat Oil Plus 2C](https://www.rubiomonocoatusa.com/products/oil-plus-2c-1-3l) hard wax finish.

The slab sits on a pair of steel fabricated and black powder coat finished legs from Flowyline Design.
