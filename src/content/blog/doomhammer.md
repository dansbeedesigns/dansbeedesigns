World of Warcraft has always been my go-to game! For the HORDE!

I originally created this prop for my own game room which was decorated with all of my World of Warcraft collectibles. Since then, I have moved and given all of my WoW treasures to my oldest son Hunter. He is now the proud owner of DoomHammer!

From start to finish, I probably have around 80 hours of work in this project. Made from a variety of materials including foam sheets, wood veneer, PVC pipe, and paint.

I applied the same weathered, earth-soaked style finish to this prop weapon that I used on my mud orc helm from The Lord of the Rings. I really like this look and it is pretty easy to achieve with liquid latex and cotton batting. Check out my post on the Uruk-Hai Helm for more details on that.
