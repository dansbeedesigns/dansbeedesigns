From deep within Middle Earth!

One of my sons is the biggest fan of the Lord of the Rings! When I decided to try my hand at making this helm, I knew it was for him from the very beginning!

Crafted from cosplay foam, liquid latex, cotton batting, and paint, mounted onto a custom-made stand made from solid walnut and laser engraved then resin filled.

This was a very rewarding experience and I'm super thrilled to know he proudly displays it alongside his other LoTR treasures!
